VLA15 is the most advanced Lyme disease vaccine candidate currently in clinical development  with two Phase 3 trials underway. It leverages an established mechanism of action  inducing antibodies to Borrelia outer surface protein A (OspA) to prevent the transmission of Lyme borreliosis from infected ticks.
