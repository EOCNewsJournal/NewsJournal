In establishing a significant US-based production facility  AMG is helping to secure a domestic source for this essential material.Only three plants in the Western world produce chrome metal — two of them now AMG's  in the United Kingdom and the United States — with the rest in China and Russia. New Castle extends that proven capability to the United States and marks the re-birth of domestic production  which the country lost when its last chrome metal plant closed in 2006.“Chrome is now being recognized at a level commensurate with its strategic importance to modern manufacturing — a long-overdue acknowledgment of its essential contribution to the industry. Chrome metal enhances the performance  durability  and heat resistance of advanced alloys.
