If European regulators hold traditional exchanges accountable for data pricing fairness  similar scrutiny could eventually reach crypto platforms operating in those jurisdictions.Crypto market makers like Wintermute and Jump Trading’s digital assets arm already navigate complex fee structures across dozens of venues.
